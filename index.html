Our Consulting Services

Specialized expertise to meet your industry's highest standards.

Certified Welding Inspections (CWI):

Certified Welding Inspection (CWI) service provides expert evaluation of welds for aircraft and industrial structures to ensure quality and safety.

NDT:

  • Develop and review Procedure and Technique Sheets.
  • Provide training and certification for trainees and NDT Level I, II, and III personnel.
  • Conduct proficiency evaluations and vision examinations for both NDT personnel and Quality Inspectors.
  • Perform Probability of Detection (POD) studies.

QA/QC Consulting

Utilizing X-rays or gamma rays to view the internal structure of components.

Nadcap audit review:

  • Gap analysis to identify areas for improvement.
  • Documentation assistance for quality manuals and procedures.
  • Staff training on NADCAP standards and audit preparation.
  • Mock audits for compliance assessment and feedback.
  • Help with corrective action plans for audit findings.
  • Ongoing consultation to ensure sustained compliance.

Ultrasonic Testing (UT)

High-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws and characterize material thickness.

Magnetic Particle Testing (MT)

Detecting surface and near-surface discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials.

Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT)

Revealing surface-breaking defects in non-porous materials through dye bleed-out.

Visual Testing (VT)

Systematic observation of surfaces to identify discontinuities without equipment.

Eddy Current Testing (ECT)

Creating and validating NDT procedures to meet specific codes, standards, and client needs.
